About Me

Background

I bounced around with ideas about what I wanted to do after high school, but when I graduated and the time came to pick something, I still wasn't sure, so I decided not to go to college despite all my friends going. I wanted to be sure about what I wanted before I threw money at it. The only idea I had at that point was graphic design, and I didn't truly understand what the path was for it. I ended up going to a conference about graphic design and web development, interviewed a graphic designer, and realized I feel pulled more towards web devlopement than graphic design. I looked into it further on my own and decided it was what I wanted to do. I tried teaching myself for a few years, and came to find I need a structured system to truly grow to my fullest potential. So here I am, at Epicodus, making my first portfolio. It's going to be a blast!
